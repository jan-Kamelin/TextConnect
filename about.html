Our Mission

TextConnect is a simple, text-only social media platform designed to allow users to share their thoughts and connect with others in a minimalist environment. Our mission is to provide a clutter-free, easy-to-use platform where content is king, and distractions are minimized.

About Us

TextConnect was created by Cameron Newhouse as a project to simplify social media interactions and focus on meaningful content. We believe in the power of words and aim to create a community where users can freely express their ideas.
